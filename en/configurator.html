Configuration

Configure base unit

The configurator uses transparent PNG layers. The housing remains the fixed zero point; auxiliary gearbox and shaft form are selected separately.

Parameters

Cover setup according to master JSON: small/large, with or without ring.
With auxiliary gearbox, a combined image of gearbox and shaft is used.
The shaft form remains the same selection criterion; with or without auxiliary gearbox only selects the image set.
Handwheel and position indicator are additional options. When the handwheel option is selected, the handwheel appears as its own layer.
Operating time is displayed but currently does not control layer selection.
The configurator supports visual preselection and the technical pre-inquiry.
