Configuration

Configure base unit

The configurator uses transparent PNG layers. The housing remains the fixed zero point; auxiliary gearbox and shaft form are selected separately.

  • Cover configuration according to master JSON: small/large, with or without ring.
  • With auxiliary gearbox, a combined image of gearbox and shaft is loaded.
  • The shaft form remains the selection criterion — with or without auxiliary gearbox only selects the image set.
  • Handwheel and position indicator are additional options.

Use configuration as pre-inquiry
