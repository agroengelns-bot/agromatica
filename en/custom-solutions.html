Custom solutions

Technical custom solutions for industrial actuator technology, valve systems and demanding mechanical engineering applications.

01 | Vibration-intensive environments

Vibration-optimized actuator systems and specially manufactured controller solutions for centrifuges, railway technology and dynamic industrial applications.

02 | Force-travel controlled valve actuator

After the defined contact force is reached, the motor is mechanically switched off in the closing direction. The holding force is not only maintained by spindle self-locking, but mechanically stored with elasticity and retained without motor current.

03 | IO-Link signal technology

Industrial signal and communication solutions for intelligent integration of sensors, actuators and automation systems.

04 | Customer-specific solutions

Custom actuator solutions for a wide range of industrial requirements – from individual units to series production. Agromatic implements adapted actuator solutions for special operating times, strokes, forces and torques, special voltages, dual-voltage inputs, shaft variants, mounting parts, customer-specific lifting units and ready-to-install factory-set actuators.
