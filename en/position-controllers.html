Position controllers for Agromatic electric actuators

Electronic position controllers for Agromatic actuators – matched to series, supply voltage, setpoint, actual-value signal and installation situation.

Control

Positioning according to an external setpoint

Position controllers allow Agromatic actuators to be integrated into customer-side control and regulation concepts. Depending on the series, different controller versions are available, for example as an internal controller board or as an external solution in the control cabinet.
