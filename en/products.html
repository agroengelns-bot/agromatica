Selection guide

Which information helps us find the right actuator?

For reliable preselection, the most important details are movement type, performance and environmental conditions. For Ex applications, zone, approval and temperature range are also required.

  • Movement type: rotary, part-turn or linear movement
  • Torque, thrust force, stroke, angle or number of turns
  • Operating time, thrust speed or desired control characteristic
  • Voltage, control signal, feedback and duty cycle
  • Environment: temperature, humidity, vibration, mounting position
  • Explosion protection: zone, equipment group, temperature class and approval

Fast orientation

Clarify the movement first: rotary/part-turn, linear, Ex Zone 2/22 or Ex Zone 1. For high force or special mechanics, we recommend direct technical coordination.
